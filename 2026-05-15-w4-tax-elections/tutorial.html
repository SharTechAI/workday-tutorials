Tutorial reproduces a typical Workday Worker self-service flow for updating federal Form W-4. Field labels ("Filing Status", "Multiple Jobs or Spouse Works", "Claim Dependents", "Other Adjustments", "Extra Withholding"), the Pay → Tax Elections navigation pattern, and the e-signature certification follow the IRS 2025 W-4 layout and Workday's published task pattern. Specific dollar deltas, the 2025 form revision, and per-pay withholding estimates illustrate the flow — actual values depend on payroll configuration and the IRS withholding tables in effect. State withholding is handled by a separate state-specific form (CT-W4 shown). Sources: IRS Form W-4 (2025) instructions, Workday Community help "Edit Federal Withholding Elections", and university job aids (e.g., Yale, Stanford, UC Workday training pages on Tax Elections).
